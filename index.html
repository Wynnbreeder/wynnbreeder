Help

Requirements for Breeding:

  • The first mount must have an average stat limit of at least 20 (no rounding up).
  • Two mount saddles are required.

Process:

  • Place both mounts in the Feeder Interface (one on the left, one in the food tray).
  • Breeding takes 6 hours, both parents are consumed.
  • A new mount is created in the slot of the first parent.

Values of the Offspring:

  • The offspring's current level will be based off of the higher of the two parents' own current levels, with some random variance applied to it.
  • Limits are based on the average of both parents plus bonus and randomization.
  • Limit values of the parents are irrelevant.

Strategy:

  • To maximize potential gain for an offspring, you should feed both parents until their limits are maxed out, and minimize the limit difference between them. Breeding mounts with a large limit difference between them can result in an offspring that is worse than the higher-limit parent used to breed it.
